## About Me

I am a full-stack and mobile developer based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I build user-focused products with a strong emphasis on clean architecture, secure backend systems, responsive interfaces, and maintainable code.

My work sits at the intersection of product thinking and engineering execution, from polished frontend experiences to scalable APIs and mobile applications.

## What I Focus On

- Building full-stack products with React, Next.js, Node.js, and Laravel.
- Developing cross-platform mobile apps with Flutter and Firebase.
- Designing systems that prioritize performance, security, and long-term maintainability.
- Turning business requirements into reliable software that ships.
